COVID-19 Update

We value you and your safety. That is why we are taking every precaution to make sure that 503 remains a safe and cozy space to gather. While inside our space, we ask that you wear a mask if you are not seated at your table. We are also making sure that common items like books and games are sanitized between uses. Our vision is for our space to be a gathering place for the community, and that cannot happen unless you feel like we are taking care of you as best we can. We are so very appreciative of those that have supported us through this time.
